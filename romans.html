ROMANS PIZZA

Roman's Pizza is a South African-based pizza restaurant chain franchise founded in 1993. Originally named Little Caesar's (copied from the American brand Little Caesars pizza) when it was founded by Arthur Nicolakakis in Pretoria. The chain was rebranded in 2002 and renamed Roman's Pizza.[2] The company is known for its two for one pizza promotions with a business model that seeks to minimize prices whilst maintaining quality
